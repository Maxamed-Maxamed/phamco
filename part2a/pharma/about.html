Our History

A Legacy of Innovation

Founded in 1985, PharmaCo began as a small research laboratory with a big vision. Our founders, Dr. Elizabeth Chen and Dr. James Parker, set out to create a company that would pioneer new approaches to treating diseases with significant unmet needs.

Over the decades, we have grown into a global biopharmaceutical company with presence in over 50 countries, but our commitment to patients and scientific excellence remains unchanged.

Our journey has been marked by numerous breakthroughs and industry-first approvals, establishing PharmaCo as a leader in innovative therapeutics across multiple disease areas.
