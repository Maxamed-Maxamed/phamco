At PharmaCo, we focus our research and development efforts on therapeutic areas where we can make the most significant impact on patients' lives. Our expertise spans several critical disease areas, with a commitment to developing innovative solutions for unmet medical needs.

Cardiology

Our cardiology portfolio addresses a range of cardiovascular conditions, from hypertension to heart failure and thrombosis.

We've pioneered several breakthrough treatments that have transformed the standard of care for heart disease, helping millions of patients worldwide.

Key Focus Areas:

  • Coronary Artery Disease
  • Heart Failure
  • Hypertension
  • Thrombotic Disorders

Featured Products:

CardioShield

Advanced therapy for reducing cardiovascular events in high-risk patients.

VascoFlow

Innovative anticoagulant with reduced bleeding risk compared to conventional therapies.

Neurology

Our neurology division is dedicated to developing treatments for complex neurological disorders that profoundly impact patients' quality of life.

We combine advanced neuroscience insights with innovative drug development approaches to address neurological conditions with significant unmet needs.

Key Focus Areas:

  • Neurodegenerative Diseases (Alzheimer's, Parkinson's)
  • Multiple Sclerosis
  • Epilepsy
  • Rare Neurological Disorders

Featured Products:

NeuroClear

Experimental therapy targeting tau protein aggregation in Alzheimer's disease.

EpiControl

Next-generation anti-seizure medication with improved tolerability profile.

Immunology

Our immunology program focuses on developing targeted therapies for autoimmune and inflammatory conditions, addressing the underlying immune dysregulation.

By understanding the complex pathways of the immune system, we're creating precision treatments that offer new hope for patients with chronic immune-mediated diseases.

Key Focus Areas:

  • Rheumatoid Arthritis
  • Inflammatory Bowel Disease
  • Psoriasis
  • Systemic Lupus Erythematosus

Featured Products:

ImmunoBalance

Biologic therapy targeting specific cytokines involved in inflammatory cascades.

AutoImmune Modulator

Novel small molecule that selectively inhibits pathogenic immune cell activation.
